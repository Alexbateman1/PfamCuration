CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: No magnetic response, no magnetosomes, no iron
CC       crystals of any sort. MamC is mislocalized in a few punctate spots
CC       throughout the cell (PubMed:24816605). No magnetic response at 30
CC       degrees Celsius, a weak response at lower temperature. At 30 degrees
CC       Celsius magnetosome vesicles are smaller, sometimes align in a chain
CC       with the filament, only a few have very small crystals. Other, possibly
CC       precursor magnetosome vesicles are visible. MamC and MamI mislocalized
CC       to 1 to a few patches in most cells (PubMed:27286560). Deletion of 3
CC       consecutive genes (mamJ, mamK, mamL) leads to cells devoid of
CC       magnetosomes or a magnetic response (PubMed:22043287). Deletion of
CC       approximately 80 kb of DNA, including this operon, leads to cells that
CC       are non-magnetic, lack internal membrane systems, grow poorly, have
